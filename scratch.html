Cosmo's Top 3 Favorite BYU Sports Nation Memories

  1. BYU's win over Michigan in the 1984 National Championship!
    • Cosmo's favorite head coach LaVell Edwards coached the team!
    • Robbie Bosco led the offense as QB!
    • BYU went 13-0 that season!
  2. Taysom Hill Ran Almost 300 Yards against UT!
    • On 9/7/23, Taysom Hill rushed for 259 yards!
    • He scored 3 rushing touchdowns!
    • Taysom Hill played so well, that less than a day after BYU beat UT, UT fired their defensive coordinator!
  3. Jimmer Fredette's 2011 Season!
    • He set a BYU Basketball record for most points in a single game with 52 points vs New Mexico!
    • He was the nation's leading scorer with an average of 28.9 points per game!
    • He drew national attention sparking "Jimmermania", eventually winning several national player of the year awards!
